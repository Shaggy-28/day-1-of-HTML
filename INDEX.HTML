Who is a Frontend Developer

A Front-End Developer is responsible for ensuring that a website or application is visually appealing, functional, and user-friendly. Leading IT companies like Oracle, IBM,

Capgemini, and many others regularly offer numerous opportunities for front-end developers. In India, the average salary for a front-end developer ranges from 7-12 LPA. This makes it an attractive and rewarding career option for aspiring tech professionals.
